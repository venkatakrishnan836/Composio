  • Developer Infra & Data — 100% buildable, 100% self-serve, 80% in SDK

⚠️ High-Risk Categories

  • AI Research & Media — Only 40% self-serve, 60% gated. Just 10% in SDK.
  • Finance & Fintech — 70% self-serve, but only 50% buildable.
  • Ecommerce — 90% self-serve, 90% buildable, but only 20% in SDK.

Action: Engage partnerships for AI Research apps. Consider RPA/webhooks for blocked platforms.

🔑 Pattern: The "Integration Gap"

  • Self-serve ≠ Buildable ≠ In SDK — Many apps are accessible but not yet integrated.
  • Ecommerce has a 70% gap between Self-serve (90%) and In SDK (20%).
  • Marketing & Social has a 30% gap (90% self-serve, 60% buildable, 60% in SDK).

Insight: Biggest opportunity: bridging the gap in Ecommerce and Marketing.

📈 MCP-Capable Breakdown

  • 20 apps are MCP-capable.
  • Appears in 9 of 10 categories, with Communications (5) and Ecommerce (4) leading.
  • All MCP-capable apps in our dataset are classified as Easy Wins.
